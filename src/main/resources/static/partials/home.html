Welcome to the Tinderflow App

This is a simple demo app based
on both Tinder (swipe left/right)
and Tensorflow (calculate weights based on datapoints)
Here's how it works

  1. click the learning button to go to the demo
  2. swipe right if you like what you see
  3. swipe left if you don't
  4. watch the stats update in real time to reflect your choices
